PROBLEMS

Imagine a world where the air stings your lungs,

the oceans are a graveyard of plastic,

and the forests—once alive with birdsong—are silent.

But this world is not yet real.
Not entirely.

There is still time to listen, to act, to care.
If we ignore the signs, this future may
become our present.
But if we choose wisely, nature will keep
breathing with us.

HOW TO

HELP

Support a sustainable future with simple actions:

Shop mindfully: pick certified eco-friendly brands and avoid single-use plastics.

Sort your waste: separate trash into categories and take it for recycling.

Cut emissions: use green energy, public transport, or electric vehicles, bicycles.

Spread the word: educate others and support green policies.

Get involved: donate or volunteer with environmental groups.
